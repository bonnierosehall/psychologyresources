Dr. Marsha Linehan

Pioneer of Dialectical Behavior Therapy (DBT)

About Marsha Linehan

Dr. Marsha Linehan is an American psychologist and professor known for developing Dialectical Behavior Therapy (DBT), a groundbreaking therapeutic approach for individuals struggling with borderline personality disorder and emotional dysregulation. Her work has transformed mental health treatment worldwide.

Contributions

  • Created Dialectical Behavior Therapy (DBT)
  • Advanced treatment for suicidal and self-harming behaviors
  • Advocated for compassionate, skills-based mental health support
  • Integrated mindfulness into evidence-based psychotherapy

Quote

“You are doing the best you can. And you need to do better.”

Legacy

Dr. Linehan’s personal journey with mental illness and her commitment to evidence-based compassion have inspired therapists and clients alike. Her courage in sharing her own story has destigmatized mental health struggles for countless people.
