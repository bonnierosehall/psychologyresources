About Psychology Resources

This site is designed to be an opportunity to remember classis psychology texts that your local library probably had in stock at one stage. If you are not lucky enough to have access to a university library, or alternatively, your university has gone far to digital, this is an opportunity to browse pioneering masterworks that deserve their place on the shelf.
