About Me

(That’s me!)

👩‍💻 Who I Am

Hi, I’m Bonnie — a lifelong student of the human mind. I’m passionate about psychology, especially person-centred and existential approaches. I admire thinkers like Carl Rogers, Marsha Linehan, and Irvin Yalom, and this website is my little tribute to their wisdom.

🎓 My Background

I’ve studied psychology and counselling, and I currently work as a case manager. I love learning, reflecting, and finding creative ways to express big ideas — even if it means building a retro website in 2025 using the style of 1996!

💖 Why This Site?

This is a nostalgic project and a heartfelt one. I wanted a quiet, old-school space to share what I’ve learned, honour the voices that have shaped me, and maybe connect with kindred spirits who find meaning in meaning-making.
